# Customer Success Onboarding & Time-to-Value (TTV) Platform

## Project Overview

The Customer Success Onboarding & Time-to-Value (TTV) Platform is a SaaS application designed to help Customer Success teams manage customer onboarding, track adoption progress, measure time-to-value, identify onboarding risks, and improve customer outcomes through proactive intervention and AI-powered recommendations.

---

## Problem Statement

Customer Success teams often lack visibility into onboarding progress, customer engagement, and time-to-value metrics. This makes it difficult to identify onboarding bottlenecks, proactively support customers, and ensure successful product adoption.

---

## Target Users

### Customer Success Manager

Needs visibility into onboarding progress, customer health, and risk indicators.

### Customer Success Leader

Needs portfolio-level insights, onboarding performance metrics, and team visibility.

### Enterprise Customer

Needs a structured onboarding journey that accelerates value realization and product adoption.

---

## Project Goals

* Improve onboarding visibility
* Track onboarding milestone completion
* Measure customer time-to-value
* Identify onboarding risks early
* Generate proactive recommendations
* Improve customer adoption outcomes
