# Customer Personas

## Persona 1: Customer Success Manager

### Overview

A Customer Success Manager (CSM) is responsible for onboarding customers, driving product adoption, monitoring account health, and ensuring customers achieve their desired business outcomes.

### Goals

* Ensure successful customer onboarding
* Reduce time-to-value (TTV)
* Increase product adoption
* Identify at-risk customers early
* Improve customer retention

### Key Responsibilities

* Manage onboarding programs
* Conduct kickoff and review meetings
* Track customer milestones
* Monitor account health
* Coordinate with Product, Support, and Engineering teams

### KPIs

* Onboarding Completion Rate
* Time-to-Value
* Product Adoption Rate
* Customer Health Score
* Renewal Rate

### Pain Points

* Limited visibility into onboarding progress
* Difficulty identifying risks early
* Manual tracking using spreadsheets
* Lack of centralized customer information

### Platform Needs

* Customer dashboard
* Onboarding tracker
* Milestone management
* Health score visibility
* Automated alerts

## Persona 2: Customer Success Director

### Overview

A Customer Success Director oversees a portfolio of customers and manages a team of Customer Success Managers.

### Goals

* Improve onboarding performance
* Increase customer retention
* Improve team productivity
* Reduce churn risk
* Drive revenue growth through customer success

### Key Responsibilities

* Manage CSM team performance
* Review portfolio health
* Monitor onboarding effectiveness
* Report customer outcomes to leadership

### KPIs

* Customer Retention Rate
* Time-to-Value
* Portfolio Health Score
* Team Performance Metrics
* Revenue Retention

### Pain Points

* Limited visibility across accounts
* Difficulty measuring onboarding success
* Reactive risk management
* Inconsistent reporting

### Platform Needs

* Executive dashboard
* Portfolio analytics
* Risk reporting
* Team performance insights

---

## Persona 3: Enterprise Customer

### Overview

An enterprise customer is purchasing and implementing a SaaS solution to solve a business problem and expects measurable value.

### Goals

* Fast implementation
* Clear onboarding process
* Rapid business value
* Successful adoption by internal users

### Key Responsibilities

* Attend onboarding sessions
* Complete onboarding tasks
* Drive internal adoption
* Measure business outcomes

### Success Metrics

* Time-to-Value
* User Adoption
* Business Outcomes Achieved
* Project Completion

### Pain Points

* Unclear onboarding steps
* Delayed implementation
* Lack of visibility into progress
* Difficulty coordinating stakeholders

### Platform Needs

* Onboarding visibility
* Milestone tracking
* Progress updates
* Clear next steps
